It is important of our commitment to recognize the brightest individuals and companies in the interior design industry through a fair and transparent process remains consistency.

A carefully selected first panel of judges consists leading estate developers and designers will assess every entry on their aesthetic excellence and technical content.
The second panel judges are selected due to their outstanding professionalism in their field, they will assess the selected entries from creativity and living experience.
Further to the industry status, MAISONZAN values the public opinions, who are the one ultimate users of design. Therefore a public vote makes up 30% of the final result. The vote is strictly monitored by IP address, with one IP address only can vote one category.
